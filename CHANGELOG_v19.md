# 3D Model Editor v19

## Animation timeline refinements
- Removed the separate upper frame slider from the Animation tab.
- Timeline ruler/track area now acts as the seekbar.
- During playback, only the timeline playhead/seekbar moves.
- Timeline can be scrubbed by clicking and dragging directly on the ruler or track lanes.
- Added Spacebar shortcut to toggle Play/Pause when not typing inside an input/select field.
